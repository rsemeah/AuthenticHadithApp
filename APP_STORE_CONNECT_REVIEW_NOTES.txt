== Account Deletion ==
Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete My Account → type "DELETE" → confirm.
All user data (profile, saved hadiths, learning progress, notes, AI history,
subscription metadata) is archived then permanently purged. The auth account
is removed. The user is signed out and cannot sign back in.

== Subscriptions ==
Settings → Manage Subscription shows available plans.
"Restore Purchases" button is at the bottom of the subscription screen.
Subscriptions use Apple In-App Purchase via RevenueCat.
No external payment links are shown on iOS.
Plans: Monthly Premium ($9.99/mo), Annual Premium ($49.99/yr), Lifetime ($99.99).

== Content Methodology ==
Authentic Hadith curates classical hadith collections from established scholarly
sources (Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, and six other canonical compilations).
AI features provide educational summaries only and do not issue religious rulings.
